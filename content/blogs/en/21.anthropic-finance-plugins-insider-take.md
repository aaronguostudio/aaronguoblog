## What Would Actually Get Adopted

A few things from the technical and hands-on evaluation:

**What moves fast:** Deliverables in known formats where the data subscription is already in place. Earnings summaries, comp tables, first-draft research notes, client-facing reports. **What moves slower:** Full models going directly into client-facing materials without review. **The real blocker is data, not tooling.** If your firm doesn't have FactSet or Capital IQ under contract, you're not unlocking the full depth of these connectors. The plugins function without the MCP data feeds â€” but it's the difference between a Bloomberg terminal with a subscription and one without. **Start the compliance conversation before you're ready to deploy.** The technical lift is lighter than most teams expect.
