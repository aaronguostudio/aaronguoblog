AI makes every idea cheap to try. So capable users often try everything: another prompt, another agent, another workflow, another experiment.

But using AI well is becoming less like operating a tool and more like managing a company. An exhausted leader gives unclear direction, changes priorities too often, and multiplies confusion across the team. AI amplifies the same pattern.

Rest is not separate from the AI productivity system. Clear judgment is part of the stack.
